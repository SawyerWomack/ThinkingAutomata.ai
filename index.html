01 / ABOUT

What Thinking Automata builds

Thinking Automata is a student-led AI development team that designs and deploys custom AI ambassadors for universities, colleges, and research communities — personas that represent your institution's identity, voice, and research at any event or platform.

We go beyond keynote talks. Your AI ambassador can open conferences, facilitate workshops, introduce research initiatives, represent your department at external events, and serve as a living, speaking face of your institution's work. You own it. It speaks for you.

Our work spans Artificial Intelligence, Agentic AI, and Cybersecurity. We handle everything: script development, avatar creation, voice synthesis, media production, and delivery — so your team doesn't have to.

We are currently accepting commissions for conferences, workshops, department and college meetings, research showcases, and large-scale institutional events. Contact us or our faculty advisor Dr. Gerry Dozier to get started.

02 / CASE STUDY

AUBRIA — Our First Deployment

The Spark

The concept was born at Auburn University through the vision of Auburn University PSFS Director Christine Cline. During the development of the 2026 AI@AU/Team Sciences: Building Research Communities in AI (Showcase & Workshop), Christine proposed opening the event with something entirely new — an AI-Generated Keynote Speaker — and shared the idea with Dr. Jennifer Kerpelman (Associate Vice President for Research), who carried it to Dr. Gerry Dozier (Faculty Advisor).

The Team

Dr. Dozier assembled a student team from the CSSE student body to bring the idea to life. He found us — a PhD student, a Master's student in Data Engineering, and a sophomore BS student. Three perspectives. One shared mission.

The Build

Under the guidance of Dr. Jennifer Kerpelman, Dr. Gerry Dozier (McCrary Professor of AI and Cybersecurity in CSSE), and Christine Cline (Director of PSFS), we developed AUBRIA — Auburn's AI keynote speaker — now available for conferences, workshops, and meetings across the university and beyond.

What's Next

The team is passionate about developing AI-Generated Keynote Talks for any conference, workshop, or meeting. We're actively working on interactive audience Q&A capabilities, and we're ready to build a custom AI keynote personality for your university or organization — contact us to get started.
